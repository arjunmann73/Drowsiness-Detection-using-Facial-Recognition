About Us

We are group students from Nanyang Technological University pursing Undergraduate degrees in Computer Engineering and Electrical Engineering.

We believe that emotions enable us to embrace life with honesty, creativity, and enthusiasm.

Finding a way to express these emotions through paintings helps discover your inner self and gives peace of mind.

Our vision is to help you find the right paintings you can use to express yourself.
